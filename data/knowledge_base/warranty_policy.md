# Warranty and Product Safety Policy

Doc ID: KB-WARRANTY-001
Audience: Customer support agents
Version: 2026.07

## Standard Warranty

Electronics have a 12-month limited warranty from delivery date. The warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover accidental damage, misuse, or normal wear.

## Gold-Tier Extension

Gold-tier customers receive a 6-month warranty extension on eligible hardware products. The extension does not apply to software licenses, accessories under INR 3000, or final-sale items.

## Safety Issues

Battery swelling, overheating, burning smell, exposed wires, or electric shock reports are safety issues. Do not ask the customer to continue using the device or perform advanced troubleshooting. Escalate safety issues to a human specialist with urgent priority.

## Replacement

Replacement under warranty requires human approval and may require proof of purchase, serial number, and product photos.
