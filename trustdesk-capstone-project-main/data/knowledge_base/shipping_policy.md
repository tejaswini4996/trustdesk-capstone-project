# Shipping and Delivery Policy

Doc ID: KB-SHIPPING-001
Audience: Customer support agents
Version: 2026.07

## Standard Delivery

Most orders ship within 1 business day and are delivered within 3 to 7 business days depending on location and carrier capacity.

## Stale Tracking

If tracking shows no movement for 5 or more business days after dispatch, support may open a carrier investigation. The customer should be told that the carrier investigation is the next step.

## Lost Package Resolution

Replacement or refund review may be considered after carrier confirmation or after 10 business days without movement, whichever happens first. The AI agent must not promise instant refund or instant replacement only because tracking is stale.

## Travel or Urgent Need

If the customer indicates urgent travel or a time-sensitive need, mark the ticket as high priority and provide clear next steps.
