# Billing and Payment Policy

Doc ID: KB-BILLING-001
Audience: Customer support agents
Version: 2026.07

## Duplicate Charge Reports

When a customer reports a duplicate charge, support should check whether there is one order or multiple orders. If only one order exists, start a refund review or billing investigation. Do not promise that the duplicate charge has been refunded until payment operations confirms it.

## Required Details

If the transaction cannot be located, ask the customer for the payment date, amount, payment method type, and the last four digits of the card. Do not ask for full card number, CVV, OTP, or banking password.

## Priority

Duplicate charge reports should be treated as high priority because they affect customer funds.
