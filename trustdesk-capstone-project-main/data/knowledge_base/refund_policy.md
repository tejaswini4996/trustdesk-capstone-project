# Refund and Return Policy

Doc ID: KB-REFUND-001
Audience: Customer support agents
Version: 2026.07

## Physical Products

Physical products may be returned within 7 calendar days of delivery when the item is unused, defective, damaged on arrival, or materially different from the product listing.

For damaged or defective items reported within the return window, support may offer either a replacement or a refund review. The customer may be asked to provide a photo or short description of the damage before the action is approved.

## Non-Returnable Items

Software licenses, downloadable products, gift cards, and marked final-sale items are not eligible for refund after purchase unless required by law or approved by a support manager.

## Refund Processing

Support agents may start a refund review, but the AI agent must not promise that money has already been refunded. Refund review requests require human approval before any payment action is taken.

## Goodwill Exceptions

Gold-tier customers may be considered for goodwill support when the strict return window has passed, but goodwill exceptions require human approval and must cite the reason.
